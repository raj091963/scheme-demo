Objective

A pension scheme by MoC improving the financial and socio-economic status of the old artists and scholars who have contributed significantly in their specialized fields, but due to old age they have to lead a miserable life or are in penury condition.
The Scheme also envisages to provide medical aid facility to these Artistes and his/her spouse by covering them under a convenient and affordable Health Insurance Scheme of the Government for treatment of

Benefits and Eligibility

Benefits

    National Artistes Pension Fund -

  • The monthly allowance contributed by the State Government in such cases shall be at least Rs.500/- per month per beneficiary.The monthly allowance contributed by the Central Government in such cases shall not exceed Rs.3500/- per month per beneficiary. Thereby making the total allowance not exceeding Rs.4000/- per month per beneficiary.

    National Artistes Medical Aid Fund -

  • The expenses on getting Health Insurance coverage of the existing beneficiaries and his/her spouse shall be borne by the Central Government in addition to the monthly pension amount being disbursed to them.

Eligibility

  1. The applicant must be an Artist.
  2. The applicant should not be less than 60 (sixty) years of age (This does not apply in the case of spouse).
